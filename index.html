Covid 19 information in the first half of 2021

The COVID-19 is an ongoing global pandemic of coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19)
caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2),
which was declared to be a Public Health Emergency of International Concern on 30 January 2020 by WHO (Wikipedia, 2021).

     Covid19 cases in the first half of 2021






In the first half of 2021, 98,971,959 new Covid19 cases were added,

35.4% of new cases are came from Asia and 25.1% of new cases are came from Europe.

  COVID-19 vaccination per Country in the
                    first half of 2021






There are 1,849,178,536 people vaccinated in the first half of 2021 all over the world,

only 23.7% of people in the world get vaccinated.

   The Top 10 countries having the largest
    number of tests in the first half of 2021






In the first half of 2021, the world conducted a total of 1,246,831,134 tests for COVID-19,

of which Asia accounted for 33.9% and Europe accounted for 41.6% .
